Type of project: UX/UI
Role: UX/UI Designer

Switch Off is a startup that created a smart device that disconnects electronic devices when they’re not in use, helping people save up to 15% on their electric bill.

The challenge

The Switch Off team launched a landing page as a first prototype to test the value proposition of their product, after a couple of months they realized that the site was not doing well, the product wasn’t clear enough for the users and the presales were not going as expected.
